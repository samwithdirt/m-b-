How we got here
01

It started on TikTok

May 2026. I was just scrolling, minding my own business, when I saw this very gorgeous girl. I liked the video and swiped. Then I landed on your reposts — and I saw something deeper. Someone who was carrying more than she showed. I came to your DMs and said, "I'm gonna help with your mental health." I don't think either of us knew that one message would change everything.

02

The WhatsApp era began

You got my number from TikTok. "Hey." "Hiii." "And you are?" — "Boipelo." From that first awkward exchange to talking every single day. You told me you usually block guys who slide into your DMs. But for some reason, you let me stay. I still flex about that, by the way.

03

The calls that lasted forever

I don't know how it happened, but our calls would go on for hours and hours. We'd talk about everything and nothing. You'd get sleepy but refuse to hang up. I'd be fighting bad WiFi and negative airtime balances but I'd still find a way to stay on the line. There's something about your voice at 3am that makes everything feel okay.

04

The fourth of July, 2026

We were on a call. I don't remember exactly what we were talking about, but I remember the moment. The silence before it. The way your voice softened. We both said "I love you" — not planned, not rehearsed, just true. July 4th, 2026 stopped being about fireworks and became about us.

05

Two people building something real

This isn't just about you or just about me. A relationship is built by two people. We've had our moments — the playful bullying (even though you get sad, meh), the late-night study sessions we barely take seriously, the Spotify blends, the voice notes, the "did you eat today?" questions. We're two imperfect people choosing each other every single day, and that's exactly what makes this beautiful.

My vows to you

I vow to always show up.

Not just when it's easy. Not just when the WiFi is good and the airtime is loaded. I'll show up when it's hard, when we're far apart, when life gets complicated. You'll never have to wonder if I'm here — because I will be.

I vow to protect your peace.

That pure, innocent soul you carry — I'll guard it like it's my own. I know you've been through things that made you build walls. I'm not here to break them down. I'm here to sit beside them until you feel safe enough to open the door.

I vow to grow with you.

Two people building together. I'm in matric, you're in grade 10 — we're both still becoming who we're meant to be. I vow to grow alongside you, not ahead of you or behind you. Side by side. UJ might be calling my name, but so are you — and I'll always find a way to answer both.

I vow to be your bully. Lovingly.

I'll tease you about your dancing. I'll call you out when you haven't eaten. I'll send you ugly screenshots. But I'll also be the first person to make sure you're okay, the first to apologise when I go too far, and the last person to ever let you feel alone.
